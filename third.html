Project Brief

Bath Salt Works is a website which lets you buy materials for factories in order to produce products that uses salt and other materials presented in the website as their main component for making: Food, Cosmetics, Pharmacy and etc.

The Problem

There are a lot of salt resources online which you can find and buy material from, but it's very hard to find a place that delivers their products in the highest quality possible.

The Solution

In order to sell the idea of "highest quality", I designed a website which give the products their energy they need and did it in the most comfortable way there is (according to other similar websites).

The Process

First I researched any website that sells the same products and delivers to the same manufactures, Then I noted every way buying I had difficult with and made a solution for them in my design so it will be more comfortable.
