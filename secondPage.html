Project Brief

Programmers.com is a web application which provides tools for Programmers to find a job in the most convenient way there is and also allows companies to find Programmers based on their needs and set of skills required.

The Problem

Today, More and more Programmers struggle to find a work based on their needs, a lot of companies do not deliver their needs precisely, which causes some Programmers to not send their application.

The Solution

Programmers.com lets companies fill their information correctly and makes the "finding a job" process to more fun and intuitive. you can find a job as an Programmer more easily and based on your and the companies needs.
