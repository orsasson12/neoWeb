Overview

Project Brief

Web app that is intended for employers who are looking for programmers and for programmers who are looking for a job, the app allows you to create a user and receive offers from several jobs according to the employer's adjustments and needs.

The Problem

A lot of applications presents where the pubs and clubs are located but to know what kind of music played we need to do some research, this process is pretty exhausting and sometimes I find myself and my friends staying at home due to not finding places that plays the music we love.

The Solution

WER app presents not only where the pubs and clubs are located at but also it present which kind of music is played there, what makes a music lover to achieve a better decision in a short amount of time.

The Process

First I did a research on other similar applications and witnessed that no application presents the type of music played in the pubs and clubs they present, I decided to design an app just for that cause (and some other benefits).
