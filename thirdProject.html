Overview

Project Brief

E-commerce website which presents the artist works and giving clients opportunities to look, feel, search and buy paintings they are related to. The feel and experience the costumer walks through should be safe and clean.

The Problem

A lot of artists share their paintings throughout the Internet but not many of them makes you feel like the artist wants you to feel. In order to create this feeling, one should recognize and deliver the artists state of mind.

The Solution

The websites presents not only the paintings done by the artist but also the way she feels about them, comfort and a place to feel safe , I delivered these feeling in a way of wireframing from looking at painting to buying them.

The Process

First I did my research on other websites and wrote down the mistakes they've made, Then I presented the client with the UX and points I think would be best for her website, later on I presented her with the design which I felt was the best for her.
